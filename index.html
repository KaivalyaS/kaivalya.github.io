Title: Quality Analysis Of Rice Grain Using Image Processing

Description: Quality Analysis of rice grain using image processing. The quality of food grain we consume is of more importance,as people are becoming educated their demand for quality of grains is increasing . There is possibility of adulteration of food grains by the traders .In the present grain-handling scenario,grain type and quality are identified manually by visual inspection which is tedious and not accurate. There is need for the growth of fast ,accurate and objective system for quality determination of food grains. In this project an image processing technique is used as an attempt to automate the process which overcome the drawback of manual process.
